# Self Credit Repair Vault

## Vision
The Self Credit Repair Vault is designed to help consumers confidently navigate the credit dispute process through education, organization, and practical tools. Rather than relying on expensive monthly credit repair services, users are equipped with structured resources, templates, and checklists to better understand their credit reports and make informed decisions.

The goal is to reduce confusion, organize the dispute process, and empower individuals to take an active role in improving the accuracy of their credit reports while understanding their consumer rights and available escalation options.

This product emphasizes education, documentation, and repeatable workflows instead of promises of guaranteed outcomes.

## Target Customer

The ideal customer:

- Has one or more negative credit report items they believe may be inaccurate or require investigation.
- Has an important financial goal approaching, such as purchasing a home, financing a vehicle, renting an apartment, or qualifying for employment or a security clearance.
- Prefers a do-it-yourself approach but wants organized guidance, templates, and practical workflows.
- Wants to better understand the credit reporting process before paying for outside services.
